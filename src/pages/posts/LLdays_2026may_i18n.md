"Excuse me! I was just logging in? Anyways I'm really confused because I can't login at all, and then I stepped on your foot - I am so sorry about this!! But can you help me login? - Uh sorry, I mean would you be kind enough to let me know how to do login!? How should I do this -"

A solid 90-degree bow - looking up, she realized that teardrops had emerged from the corners of her eyes...

Because she's been really anxious - 

It was because.

Today was the first day of high school.

Having failed the high school entrance exam because she was abysmal at math, she might not be able to continue being high schooler if she carried on - "what should I do please oh god my future is already -" - just as she was thinking that, like a helping hand from god, like Kandata’s spider thread from the story she read about in class - "There's a school that even I can go to!" - she miraculously became a high school student! She thought, but -

Once again she's failing to enter the entrance, why would it be - wait this isn't a joke right, this -

Then this happened.

"Login? Wait, I've never heard of anyone who can't login. And you're so worried you stepped on my foot? Sorry I've completely lost you - "

She replied with a very confused voice.

"Ah - no, sorry, Stepping on your foot and logging in are two different topics - "

Then she explained, panicking while raising her face.

Not knowing if she was avoiding my desperate face, beyond those subtle glimpses -

"Anyways. Isn't this the public homepage of our school? It does not look like our student portal at all? What happened? Don't tell me that you -"

"I haven't really used a computer before!!"

"..."

She stopped.

No way! Did I say anything wrong?

"I have just been using my phone my entire life, so -"

"... Then how about just using your phone? Surely you can login from your mobile app. So -"

Just as she calmed down and answered steadily, "oh no, oh no, oh no -" Suddenly,  with both of her hands she firmly grasped hers.

"Excuse me -"

"Sorry! I might have confused you but I'm really worried - my foot was on the verge of dancing! Today when I entered the room, a tutor told me that it would be better to login from a desktop computer - if I can't do such a simple thing, I, I - I might just be expelled!"

"Huh? That won't happen okay? By the way I really have no idea what you are talking about - dancing, or whatever - forget about it. Anyways, could you please just let go of my hand - "

"No!"

"No!? - Are you a primary school student!?"
